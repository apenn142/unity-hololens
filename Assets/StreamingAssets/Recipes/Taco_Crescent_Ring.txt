#Ingredients
1,lb.,ground beef
1,package,taco seasoning mix
1,cup,shredded cheddar cheese
2,cans (8 oz each),Pillsbury refrigerated crescent dinner rolls
shredded lettuce
chopped tomatoes
sliced ripe olives
taco sauce or salsa
#Steps
Heat oven to 375F. Cook beef  in 10 inch skillet until no longer pink.
Add taco seasoning mix and 1/2 cup of water. Simmer 3 to 4 minutes until slightly thickened.
Mix beef mixture and cheese in a medium sized bowl.
Unroll both cans of dough and separate in 16 triangles, arrange triangles in a ring on cooking sheet.
Spoon beef mixture on the half of each triangle closest to the center of the ring.
Take remaining half of triangle and overlap it over the beef mixture.
Bake 20 to 25 minutes until dough is golden brown. Cool 5 to 10 minutes.
#Servings
8
#Info
Great for large parties!
